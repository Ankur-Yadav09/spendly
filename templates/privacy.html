Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here is how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including your email address, name, and the financial transaction data you choose to log in the app (amounts, categories, and descriptions).

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used exclusively to provide the expense tracking service, generate your spending reports, and maintain your user account. We do not use your data for marketing or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

We do not share your personal financial data with third-party service providers. If we integrate any external tools in the future, we will update this policy and notify you.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com.
